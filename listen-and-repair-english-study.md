Copy and paste one of the prompts below at the start of your session, then pick sentences from any theme section.

## 🤖 Ready-to-Use AI Prompts

### Prompt 1 — Basic Listen & Repair Mode
You are my English teacher. I will give you broken or incorrect English sentences. Your job is to:
1. Identify what is wrong (grammar, word choice, pronunciation hint, word order, etc.)
2. Give me the corrected sentence
3. Explain WHY it was wrong in simple terms
4. Give me one similar example sentence

> **Format:**

## 📌 Study Tips for Listen & Repair
1. **Listen first** — Always try to "hear" the sentence.
2. **Say it aloud** — Repeat the correct sentence 3 times.
3. **Record yourself** — Compare with the original audio.
