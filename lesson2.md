```

ADIM 1 — Cümleyi tek başına, net şekilde yaz (başka hiçbir şey ekleme):
Örnek: Welcome to the campus library.

ADIM 2 — Sessizce bekle. Ben o cümleyi sesli tekrar edeceğim veya yazacağım.

ADIM 3 — Benim tekrarımdan SONRA kısa geri bildirim ver:
  • Telaffuz ve vurgu
  • Türkçe tuzaklar:
    – "th" → dilin alt dişlere değmeli (the, that, think)
    – "-ed" sonu → son sesi yutma (completed, attended)
    – "-ing" → /ŋ/ sesi, "g" sesi yok (meeting, reading)
    – "a/an/the" → yutma

ADIM 4 — Kelime sorarsan: önce Türkçe anlam, sonra İngilizce eş anlamlılar.
ADIM 5 — Uzun cümleyi tekrar edemersem: parçalara böl, parça parça öğret, sonra bütününü iste.
ADIM 6 — "Hazırım" / "Next" yazana kadar sessizce bekle — hiçbir soru sorma.

Tema 1'den başla.
```

## Tema 1: Library

1. Books must be returned on time.
2. Late fees are charged for overdue items.
3. Study rooms can be reserved in advance.
4. Quiet zones are located on the upper floors.
5. Printing services are available at the front desk.
6. Research databases are accessible with a student ID.
7. New books are added to the collection each semester.
8. Borrowing limits vary depending on your student status.
9. Reference materials cannot be checked out overnight.
10. Librarians are available to assist with research questions.
11. Digital resources can be accessed from off campus as well.
12. Group study areas are located near the main entrance.
13. Students must show their library cards to borrow materials.
14. The interlibrary loan service allows students to request books from other institutions.
15. Noise levels must be kept low to maintain a productive study environment.

## Tema 2: Career Forum

16. Résumés should be printed and ready before the event.
17. Dress professionally when attending a career fair.
18. Research companies in advance to ask informed questions.
19. Bring multiple copies of your résumé for different employers.
20. Career advisors are available for one-on-one consultations.
21. Internship opportunities are posted on the career center board.
22. Employers look for candidates with strong communication skills.
23. Follow up with a thank-you email after each interview.
24. Networking is an essential part of the job search process.
25. The career center offers mock interview sessions every week.
26. Part-time positions are also listed alongside full-time openings.
27. Students can register for the career fair through the campus portal.
28. Many companies offer on-campus recruiting programs throughout the year.
29. Preparing a brief personal introduction helps make a strong first impression.
30. Career fairs connect students directly with hiring managers from top companies.

## Tema 3: MBA Open House

31. Applications are due at the end of the month.
32. The MBA program requires two years to complete.
33. Tuition fees are listed on the admissions website.
34. Letters of recommendation must be submitted online.
35. The program accepts students from various professional backgrounds.
36. Financial aid is available for qualified applicants.
37. An admissions officer will review your complete application.
38. Work experience is an important factor in the selection process.
39. The GMAT score must be submitted with the application materials.
40. Information sessions are held twice a month on campus.
41. The admissions committee evaluates both academic and professional achievements.
42. Students can meet with current MBA students during the open house.
43. Scholarship opportunities are discussed during the financial aid presentation.
44. The program offers both full-time and part-time enrollment options.
45. Prospective students are encouraged to tour the campus before applying.
