## Tema 1: Kütüphane (Library)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Bir kütüphane; raflar arası, danışma bankosu, sessiz çalışma alanları.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Books must be returned on time.
2. **(8 sn)** Late fees are charged for overdue items.
3. **(10 sn)** Research databases are accessible with a student ID.
4. **(10 sn)** Borrowing limits vary depending on your student status.
5. **(10 sn)** Librarians are available to assist with research questions.
6. **(12 sn)** The interlibrary loan service allows students to request books from other institutions.
7. **(12 sn)** Noise levels must be kept low to maintain a productive study environment.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Study rooms can be reserved in advance.
9. Quiet zones are located on the upper floors.
10. Printing services are available at the front desk.
11. New books are added to the collection each semester.
12. Reference materials cannot be checked out overnight.
13. Digital resources can be accessed from off campus as well.
14. Group study areas are located near the main entrance.
15. Students must show their library cards to borrow materials.

## Tema 2: Kariyer Fuarı (Career Forum)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Bir kariyer fuarı; şirket standları, broşürler, görüşen öğrenciler.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Dress professionally when attending a career fair.
2. **(8 sn)** Career advisors are available for one-on-one consultations.
3. **(10 sn)** Bring multiple copies of your résumé for different employers.
4. **(10 sn)** Internship opportunities are posted on the career center board.
5. **(10 sn)** Follow up with a thank-you email after each interview.
6. **(12 sn)** Preparing a brief personal introduction helps make a strong first impression.
7. **(12 sn)** Career fairs connect students directly with hiring managers from top companies.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Résumés should be printed and ready before the event.
9. Research companies in advance to ask informed questions.
10. Employers look for candidates with strong communication skills.
11. Networking is an essential part of the job search process.
12. The career center offers mock interview sessions every week.
13. Part-time positions are also listed alongside full-time openings.
14. Students can register for the career fair through the campus portal.
15. Many companies offer on-campus recruiting programs throughout the year.

## Tema 3: MBA Açık Kapı / Bilgi Toplantısı (MBA Open House)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Bir MBA tanıtım günü; kayıt masası, sunum salonu, danışmanlar.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Letters of recommendation must be submitted online.
2. **(8 sn)** Financial aid is available for qualified applicants.
3. **(10 sn)** Applications are due at the end of the month.
4. **(10 sn)** Information sessions are held twice a month on campus.
5. **(10 sn)** The admissions committee evaluates both academic and professional achievements.
6. **(12 sn)** Prospective students are encouraged to tour the campus before applying.
7. **(12 sn)** Students can meet with current MBA students during the open house.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. The MBA program requires two years to complete.
9. Tuition fees are listed on the admissions website.
10. The program accepts students from various professional backgrounds.
11. An admissions officer will review your complete application.
12. Work experience is an important factor in the selection process.
13. The GMAT score must be submitted with the application materials.
14. Scholarship opportunities are discussed during the financial aid presentation.
15. The program offers both full-time and part-time enrollment options.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma. AI bunları sayar.
2. **Thought Groups** — "Although", "Because" sonrası ve virgülde doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" kelimelerini vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme (books ≠ book).
5. **Düz tonlama** — Robot gibi konuşma; İngilizce ritimli bir dildir.
