## Tema 1: Yurt ve Konut Ofisi (Campus Housing Office)

1. Housing applications for the upcoming fall semester open in early March.
2. Roommate preferences can be submitted through the online housing portal before the deadline.
3. Residence hall contracts are binding for the duration of the full academic year.
4. Early move-in before the official date is available for an additional fee.
5. Room changes are not permitted during the first two weeks of the semester.
6. Overnight guests may stay in the residence hall for no more than three consecutive nights.
7. Quiet hours are enforced from ten at night until eight in the morning on weekdays.
8. All residents must present their student ID to enter any campus residence hall.
9. Cooking is permitted only in the designated kitchen areas within each building.
10. Maintenance issues must be reported through the online work order system promptly.
11. The housing office assigns rooms based on the date and time the application was received.
12. Students who wish to live off campus must notify the housing office in writing.
13. University-owned furniture and property may not be removed from residence hall rooms.
14. Fire safety inspections are conducted without prior notice at various points throughout the year.
15. Residents must check out with their residential advisor before leaving for any extended break.

## Tema 2: Kampüs Güvenliği (Campus Safety)

16. The campus safety office operates twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week.
17. Emergency call boxes are installed at regular intervals throughout the campus grounds.
18. Students should report any suspicious activity to campus safety immediately.
19. Lost or stolen property must be reported to the campus safety office as soon as possible.
20. Campus safety officers conduct regular patrols of all parking lots and academic buildings.
21. The safe walk program provides student escorts across campus during late evening hours.
22. Bicycles must be registered with campus safety and secured to designated bike racks only.
23. Alcohol consumption is not permitted in common areas or residence hall corridors.
24. All campus incidents are documented and reviewed by the safety committee on a monthly basis.
25. Emergency notifications are sent to all registered phones through the campus alert system.
26. First aid kits are mounted in every academic building near the main stairwell entrances.
27. Students are strongly encouraged to register for the campus emergency notification system.
28. Campus safety works closely with local law enforcement on all serious or ongoing incidents.
29. Students may request a safety escort to their vehicle by contacting the campus safety office.
30. Security cameras are monitored continuously in all public areas of the university campus.

---

## Tema 3: Kampüs Ulaşımı (Campus Transit)

31. The campus shuttle runs every fifteen minutes during regular weekday operating hours.
32. Bus schedules are posted at each stop and updated in the university transit application.
33. Students with a valid university ID ride all campus shuttles free of charge.
34. The last shuttle departs from the main terminal at midnight on weeknights.
35. Extended shuttle service is provided during finals week and major campus events.
36. Bike-sharing stations are available near the main library and the student union building.
37. Carpooling programs are coordinated through the campus transportation office each semester.
38. A valid parking permit is required for all vehicles parked in designated campus areas.
39. Accessible parking spaces require a valid state-issued placard or university-issued permit.
40. The transit office offers a subsidized city bus pass to all currently enrolled students.
41. Shuttle routes and schedules are subject to change at the beginning of each semester.
42. Real-time shuttle tracking is available through the official university mobile application.
43. Passengers are asked to give priority seating to those with disabilities or heavy luggage.
44. Scooters, skateboards, and bicycles are prohibited inside all campus buildings and walkways.
45. Shuttle delays and service disruptions can be reported using the online transit feedback form.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma.
2. **Thought Groups** — virgülden sonra doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme.
5. **Düz tonlama** — cümle sonunda doğal iniş-çıkış yap.
