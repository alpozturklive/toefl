## Tema 1: Yurt ve Konut Ofisi (Campus Housing Office)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Kampüs konut ofisi; başvuru masası, oda anahtarları, planlar.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Housing applications for the upcoming fall semester open in early March.
2. **(8 sn)** Early move-in before the official date is available for an additional fee.
3. **(10 sn)** Residence hall contracts are binding for the duration of the full academic year.
4. **(10 sn)** Room changes are not permitted during the first two weeks of the semester.
5. **(10 sn)** All residents must present their student ID to enter any campus residence hall.
6. **(12 sn)** Quiet hours are enforced from ten at night until eight in the morning on weekdays.
7. **(12 sn)** The housing office assigns rooms based on the date and time the application was received.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Roommate preferences can be submitted through the online housing portal before the deadline.
9. Overnight guests may stay in the residence hall for no more than three consecutive nights.
10. Cooking is permitted only in the designated kitchen areas within each building.
11. Maintenance issues must be reported through the online work order system promptly.
12. Students who wish to live off campus must notify the housing office in writing.
13. University-owned furniture and property may not be removed from residence hall rooms.
14. Fire safety inspections are conducted without prior notice at various points throughout the year.
15. Residents must check out with their residential advisor before leaving for any extended break.

## Tema 2: Kampüs Güvenliği (Campus Safety)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Kampüs güvenlik birimi; güvenlik masası, kameralar, acil telefon.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Students should report any suspicious activity to campus safety immediately.
2. **(8 sn)** Emergency call boxes are installed at regular intervals throughout the campus grounds.
3. **(10 sn)** The safe walk program provides student escorts across campus during late evening hours.
4. **(10 sn)** Emergency notifications are sent to all registered phones through the campus alert system.
5. **(10 sn)** Security cameras are monitored continuously in all public areas of the university campus.
6. **(12 sn)** Students may request a safety escort to their vehicle by contacting the campus safety office.
7. **(12 sn)** Lost or stolen property must be reported to the campus safety office as soon as possible.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. The campus safety office operates twenty-four hours a day, seven days a week.
9. Campus safety officers conduct regular patrols of all parking lots and academic buildings.
10. Bicycles must be registered with campus safety and secured to designated bike racks only.
11. Alcohol consumption is not permitted in common areas or residence hall corridors.
12. All campus incidents are documented and reviewed by the safety committee on a monthly basis.
13. First aid kits are mounted in every academic building near the main stairwell entrances.
14. Students are strongly encouraged to register for the campus emergency notification system.
15. Campus safety works closely with local law enforcement on all serious or ongoing incidents.

## Tema 3: Kampüs Ulaşımı (Campus Transit)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Kampüs ulaşımı; durakta servis otobüsü, güzergâh tabelası.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Carpooling programs are coordinated through the campus transportation office each semester.
2. **(8 sn)** Accessible parking spaces require a valid state-issued placard or university-issued permit.
3. **(10 sn)** Scooters, skateboards, and bicycles are prohibited inside all campus buildings and walkways.
4. **(10 sn)** Students with a valid university ID ride all campus shuttles free of charge.
5. **(10 sn)** Bike-sharing stations are available near the main library and the student union building.
6. **(12 sn)** Passengers are asked to give priority seating to those with disabilities or heavy luggage.
7. **(12 sn)** Shuttle delays and service disruptions can be reported using the online transit feedback form.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. The campus shuttle runs every fifteen minutes during regular weekday operating hours.
9. Bus schedules are posted at each stop and updated in the university transit application.
10. The last shuttle departs from the main terminal at midnight on weeknights.
11. Extended shuttle service is provided during finals week and major campus events.
12. A valid parking permit is required for all vehicles parked in designated campus areas.
13. The transit office offers a subsidized city bus pass to all currently enrolled students.
14. Shuttle routes and schedules are subject to change at the beginning of each semester.
15. Real-time shuttle tracking is available through the official university mobile application.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma. AI bunları sayar.
2. **Thought Groups** — "Although", "Because" sonrası ve virgülde doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" kelimelerini vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme (books ≠ book).
5. **Düz tonlama** — Robot gibi konuşma; İngilizce ritimli bir dildir.
