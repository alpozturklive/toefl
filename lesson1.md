## Tema 1: Kampüs Yemekhanesi (Campus Dining)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Üniversite yemekhanesi; tezgâhta sıcak yemekler, içecek istasyonu, ödeme bankosu.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Used trays must be returned after finishing meals.
2. **(8 sn)** Students can use their campus cards for discounts.
3. **(10 sn)** Affordable prices are clearly shown on the dining menu.
4. **(10 sn)** Busy periods usually occur around noon and early evening.
5. **(10 sn)** Fresh meals are prepared and served throughout the day.
6. **(12 sn)** Long lines often form in the dining hall during peak hours.
7. **(12 sn)** Food from the dining hall must not be taken into classrooms or libraries.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Hot meals and cold drinks are served here daily.
9. Fresh dishes are prepared in the kitchen every morning.
10. A variety of beverages is also available at the station.
11. Several menu options are offered and rotated each day.
12. Different meals are displayed at the counter for students.
13. Payment is completed at the register near the front counter.
14. Campus cards and mobile payments are accepted at all stations.
15. Healthy choices are clearly marked on the food stations.

## Tema 2: Kampüs Mağazası (Campus Store)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Kampüs mağazası; raflarda kitaplar, logolu tişörtler, kasada öğrenciler.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Some designs feature official university branding.
2. **(8 sn)** Caps and bags are displayed on shelves.
3. **(10 sn)** University logos are printed on shirts, mugs, and accessories.
4. **(10 sn)** These products help promote school identity and community pride.
5. **(10 sn)** Notebooks and stationery are located near the back corner.
6. **(12 sn)** The store offers a variety of branded apparel and accessories.
7. **(12 sn)** Refunds are accepted within two weeks with a valid receipt.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Various school supplies and branded items are sold here.
9. Required textbooks are available for purchase in the campus store.
10. Sweatshirts and hoodies featuring the school crest are sold here.
11. Clothing sizes are available in different ranges.
12. Seasonal items are displayed in special sections.
13. Students often buy clothing as souvenirs or gifts.
14. New designs are introduced at the beginning of each semester.
15. Certain clothing items are available only for a limited time.

## Tema 3: Derslik / Amfi (Lecture Hall)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Basamaklı bir amfi; önde kürsü ve projeksiyon perdesi, sıralarda öğrenciler.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Students listen and take notes.
2. **(8 sn)** Important terms are repeated for clarity.
3. **(10 sn)** Information is presented in a structured sequence.
4. **(10 sn)** Complex topics are explained using examples with clear comparisons.
5. **(10 sn)** Attendance is recorded at the start of every session.
6. **(12 sn)** All lectures in this auditorium are expected to begin on time.
7. **(12 sn)** The instructor stands at the front podium to face the class.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Students are seated in tiered rows throughout the lecture hall.
9. Slides, charts, and graphs are projected on the main screen.
10. Key points and definitions are explained clearly during the lecture.
11. Visual materials help explain complex concepts.
12. The instructor pauses to check understanding.
13. Some students ask questions during the lecture.
14. The content is organized into clear sections.
15. The syllabus outlines all required readings for the semester.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma. AI bunları sayar.
2. **Thought Groups** — "Although", "Because" sonrası ve virgülde doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" kelimelerini vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme (books ≠ book).
5. **Düz tonlama** — Robot gibi konuşma; İngilizce ritimli bir dildir.
