## Tema 1: Kayıt Ofisi (Registrar's Office)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Öğrenci işleri (Registrar); kayıt bankosu, transkript talepleri.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Students must meet with their advisor before registering for classes.
2. **(8 sn)** Official transcripts can be requested through the registrar's online portal.
3. **(10 sn)** A minimum of twelve credit hours is required for full-time student status.
4. **(10 sn)** Name and address changes must be submitted in writing to the registrar's office.
5. **(10 sn)** Incomplete grades must be resolved within one semester of the original course ending.
6. **(12 sn)** The deadline to add or drop a course is the end of the second week.
7. **(12 sn)** Final grades are posted to the student portal within two weeks after the semester ends.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Course registration opens two weeks before the start of each semester.
9. A hold on your account will prevent you from enrolling in courses.
10. Withdrawing from a course after the deadline results in a W on your transcript.
11. Enrollment verification letters are available at the registrar's office upon request.
12. Degree audit reports show exactly which graduation requirements have been fulfilled.
13. Students may not enroll in more than eighteen credit hours without written approval.
14. Transfer credits are evaluated jointly by the registrar and the relevant academic department.
15. The official academic calendar lists all important registration dates and deadlines for the year.

## Tema 2: Akademik Danışmanlık (Academic Advising)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Akademik danışmanlık ofisi; danışman masası, ders planı belgeleri.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Advising appointments can be scheduled through the online booking system.
2. **(8 sn)** Academic probation is assigned to students whose GPA falls below a 2.0.
3. **(10 sn)** The academic advising center is open Monday through Friday during regular business hours.
4. **(10 sn)** Course substitutions and waivers must be reviewed and approved by the advising office.
5. **(10 sn)** Double majors must independently fulfill the requirements of each program they are pursuing.
6. **(12 sn)** Students may request a different advisor if they change their major or area of concentration.
7. **(12 sn)** Students on academic warning should contact their advisor within the first week of the semester.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Students are strongly encouraged to meet with their advisor at least once per semester.
9. A change of major requires written approval from both the current and new departments.
10. Prerequisites must be completed before a student may enroll in upper-division courses.
11. The advisor can help students plan a course schedule that satisfies graduation requirements.
12. Students considering graduate school should discuss their plans with an advisor early on.
13. An advising hold means a mandatory appointment is required before registration can proceed.
14. Transfer students should schedule an advising appointment during their very first week on campus.
15. Four-year degree plans are available to help students stay on track toward timely graduation.

## Tema 3: Öğrenci Destek Merkezi (Tutoring & Writing Center)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Özel ders (tutoring) merkezi; çalışma masaları, eğitmenler, öğrenciler.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Walk-in tutoring sessions are offered during peak hours on weekday afternoons.
2. **(8 sn)** The writing center provides detailed feedback on drafts, not final-stage proofreading.
3. **(10 sn)** Online tutoring sessions are available for students enrolled in fully remote programs.
4. **(10 sn)** Regular use of tutoring services is associated with stronger academic performance over time.
5. **(10 sn)** The writing center helps students improve clarity, organization, and argumentation in their papers.
6. **(12 sn)** Appointments at the tutoring center can be booked online up to one week in advance.
7. **(12 sn)** Students can request a tutor who specializes in a specific subject area or required course.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Tutoring services are available free of charge to all currently enrolled students.
9. Students should bring their assignment guidelines when visiting the writing center.
10. Group tutoring sessions are available for introductory mathematics and science courses.
11. Tutors are trained undergraduate and graduate students from across the academic departments.
12. The tutoring center is located on the second floor of the student services building.
13. Missed appointments without prior cancellation will result in a temporary hold on tutoring access.
14. The supplemental instruction program offers weekly group review sessions for difficult core courses.
15. All tutoring and writing center services are confidential and designed to support student success.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma. AI bunları sayar.
2. **Thought Groups** — "Although", "Because" sonrası ve virgülde doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" kelimelerini vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme (books ≠ book).
5. **Düz tonlama** — Robot gibi konuşma; İngilizce ritimli bir dildir.
