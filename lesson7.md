## Tema 1: Teknoloji / IT Hizmetleri (Campus Technology)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Kampüs teknoloji alanı; bilgisayarlar, projeksiyon, yazılım ekranları.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Students receive a university email account upon enrollment.
2. **(8 sn)** Two-factor authentication is required for all university accounts.
3. **(10 sn)** Computer labs are available for walk-in use during operating hours.
4. **(10 sn)** Cybersecurity workshops are offered each semester by the IT department.
5. **(10 sn)** The university provides assistive technology tools for students with disabilities.
6. **(12 sn)** Students are responsible for backing up their own academic work regularly.
7. **(12 sn)** IT support can be reached by phone, email, or in-person at the tech center.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. The campus Wi-Fi network is available in all academic buildings.
9. The IT help desk is open weekdays from eight to six.
10. Software licenses for Microsoft Office are free for enrolled students.
11. Printing credits are loaded onto the student account each semester.
12. Personal devices must be registered to access the secure campus network.
13. The learning management system is used to submit all assignments online.
14. Unauthorized software installation on lab computers is strictly prohibited.
15. Cloud storage is provided to all students through the university portal.

## Tema 2: Çevrimiçi Dersler (Online & Hybrid Classes)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Çevrim içi ders ortamı; dizüstü ekranında video konferans, sohbet penceresi.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Webcams are required for proctored online exams.
2. **(8 sn)** Discussion board participation counts toward the overall course grade.
3. **(10 sn)** Assignment deadlines remain the same regardless of the course format.
4. **(10 sn)** Academic integrity policies apply equally to online and in-person courses.
5. **(10 sn)** Online course materials are posted before the start of each week.
6. **(12 sn)** Students are expected to log in and participate actively each week.
7. **(12 sn)** Hybrid courses meet in person twice a week and online for the rest.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Video lectures must be watched before the weekly live session.
9. Technical difficulties should be reported to the instructor promptly.
10. Time zone differences must be considered when scheduling live sessions.
11. Online students have full access to the digital library and databases.
12. Students should test their audio and video before each virtual class.
13. Chat features during live sessions should be used respectfully.
14. Office hours for online courses are held through video conferencing tools.
15. Course evaluations are submitted electronically at the end of the semester.

## Tema 3: Mali Yardım / Burs (Financial Aid)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Mali yardım ofisi; danışma masası, başvuru formları, broşürler.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Work-study positions are available through the financial aid office.
2. **(8 sn)** Loan repayment begins six months after graduation or withdrawal.
3. **(10 sn)** Grants do not need to be repaid, unlike student loans.
4. **(10 sn)** Appeals for additional aid can be submitted with supporting documentation.
5. **(10 sn)** Emergency funds are available for students facing unexpected financial hardship.
6. **(12 sn)** Aid packages are reviewed and renewed at the start of each academic year.
7. **(12 sn)** Tax documents related to scholarships are mailed at the beginning of each year.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. The financial aid application must be submitted before the deadline.
9. Scholarship awards are based on both academic merit and financial need.
10. Students must maintain satisfactory academic progress to keep their aid.
11. The financial aid office holds information sessions every fall.
12. Outside scholarships must be reported to the financial aid office.
13. The estimated cost of attendance includes tuition, housing, and living expenses.
14. Students receiving aid must enroll in a minimum number of credit hours.
15. Financial literacy workshops help students manage budgets and student debt.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma. AI bunları sayar.
2. **Thought Groups** — "Although", "Because" sonrası ve virgülde doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" kelimelerini vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme (books ≠ book).
5. **Düz tonlama** — Robot gibi konuşma; İngilizce ritimli bir dildir.
