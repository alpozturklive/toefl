## Tema 1: Ders / Amfi (Science Lecture)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Bir fen dersliği; tahtada formüller, projeksiyon, öğrenci sıraları.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** The lecture covers the fundamentals of cellular biology.
2. **(8 sn)** Attendance is tracked using the digital check-in system.
3. **(10 sn)** The professor will post the lecture slides after each class.
4. **(10 sn)** Students may not use laptops unless permitted by the instructor.
5. **(10 sn)** The course requires students to submit weekly reading responses online.
6. **(12 sn)** Students who miss class are responsible for obtaining notes from a classmate.
7. **(12 sn)** The professor encouraged students to visit the tutoring center for additional support.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Students are expected to complete the assigned readings beforehand.
9. Office hours are held twice a week in the faculty building.
10. Extra credit assignments are announced during the last week of class.
11. The midterm exam covers all material from the first six weeks.
12. The final grade is based on three exams and one research paper.
13. Academic dishonesty results in automatic failure for the course.
14. Guest speakers from industry are invited each semester.
15. Discussion sections are held separately from the main lecture.

## Tema 2: Laboratuvar (Lab)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Bir laboratuvar; tezgâhlarda cihazlar, güvenlik gözlükleri, çeker ocak.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Lab coats are required before handling any chemicals.
2. **(8 sn)** Equipment should be calibrated before each experiment begins.
3. **(10 sn)** Safety goggles must be worn at all times in the lab.
4. **(10 sn)** Waste disposal procedures are posted on the wall above each station.
5. **(10 sn)** Students must complete the safety training before their first lab session.
6. **(12 sn)** All reagents must be returned to their proper storage locations after use.
7. **(12 sn)** The ventilation system must be running whenever volatile chemicals are in use.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. All spills must be reported to the lab supervisor immediately.
9. Data must be recorded in the official lab notebook.
10. Gloves are provided at the supply station near the entrance.
11. Food and drinks are strictly prohibited inside the laboratory.
12. Broken glassware must be disposed of in the designated sharps container.
13. Results should be reviewed and signed off by the lab instructor.
14. The lab is open for independent research during posted hours only.
15. Students are paired in groups of two for all experimental procedures.

## Tema 3: Araştırma / Tez (Research)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Bir araştırma/tez ortamı; ekranda veri grafikleri, makaleler, notlar.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Data collection methods should be described in detail.
2. **(8 sn)** Peer review ensures that research meets academic standards.
3. **(10 sn)** The conclusion summarizes the key findings and their implications.
4. **(10 sn)** Findings should be compared with existing research in the field.
5. **(10 sn)** Sources must be cited according to the required citation format.
6. **(12 sn)** Research proposals must be approved by the academic committee in advance.
7. **(12 sn)** The final thesis must be submitted in both digital and printed formats.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. The hypothesis must be clearly stated in the introduction.
9. The literature review summarizes previous studies on the topic.
10. The sample size must be large enough to produce reliable results.
11. Statistical analysis is used to interpret the experimental data.
12. Limitations of the study must be acknowledged in the discussion section.
13. The abstract provides a brief overview of the entire paper.
14. Funding sources must be disclosed in the acknowledgments section.
15. Revisions are often required before a paper is accepted for publication.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma. AI bunları sayar.
2. **Thought Groups** — "Although", "Because" sonrası ve virgülde doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" kelimelerini vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme (books ≠ book).
5. **Düz tonlama** — Robot gibi konuşma; İngilizce ritimli bir dildir.
