## Tema 1: Science Lecture

1. The lecture covers the fundamentals of cellular biology.
2. Students are expected to complete the assigned readings beforehand.
3. The professor will post the lecture slides after each class.
4. Attendance is tracked using the digital check-in system.
5. Office hours are held twice a week in the faculty building.
6. Extra credit assignments are announced during the last week of class.
7. The midterm exam covers all material from the first six weeks.
8. Students may not use laptops unless permitted by the instructor.
9. The final grade is based on three exams and one research paper.
10. Academic dishonesty results in automatic failure for the course.
11. Guest speakers from industry are invited each semester.
12. Discussion sections are held separately from the main lecture.
13. The course requires students to submit weekly reading responses online.
14. Students who miss class are responsible for obtaining notes from a classmate.
15. The professor encouraged students to visit the tutoring center for additional support.

---

## Tema 2: Lab

16. Safety goggles must be worn at all times in the lab.
17. Lab coats are required before handling any chemicals.
18. All spills must be reported to the lab supervisor immediately.
19. Equipment should be calibrated before each experiment begins.
20. Data must be recorded in the official lab notebook.
21. Gloves are provided at the supply station near the entrance.
22. Waste disposal procedures are posted on the wall above each station.
23. Students must complete the safety training before their first lab session.
24. Food and drinks are strictly prohibited inside the laboratory.
25. Broken glassware must be disposed of in the designated sharps container.
26. Results should be reviewed and signed off by the lab instructor.
27. The lab is open for independent research during posted hours only.
28. All reagents must be returned to their proper storage locations after use.
29. Students are paired in groups of two for all experimental procedures.
30. The ventilation system must be running whenever volatile chemicals are in use.

---

## Tema 3: Research

31. The hypothesis must be clearly stated in the introduction.
32. The literature review summarizes previous studies on the topic.
33. Data collection methods should be described in detail.
34. The sample size must be large enough to produce reliable results.
35. Statistical analysis is used to interpret the experimental data.
36. Findings should be compared with existing research in the field.
37. Limitations of the study must be acknowledged in the discussion section.
38. Sources must be cited according to the required citation format.
39. The abstract provides a brief overview of the entire paper.
40. Peer review ensures that research meets academic standards.
41. Funding sources must be disclosed in the acknowledgments section.
42. The conclusion summarizes the key findings and their implications.
43. Revisions are often required before a paper is accepted for publication.
44. Research proposals must be approved by the academic committee in advance.
45. The final thesis must be submitted in both digital and printed formats.
