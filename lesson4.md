## Tema 1: Health Center

1. Walk-in appointments are available on weekday mornings.
2. Students must present their insurance cards at check-in.
3. Prescription refills can be requested through the online portal.
4. The health center is closed on university holidays.
5. Mental health counseling is offered free of charge to enrolled students.
6. Vaccinations are required before moving into campus housing.
7. Annual physical exams are recommended for all students.
8. Test results are typically available within three to five business days.
9. Emergency cases should be directed to the nearest hospital emergency room.
10. The pharmacy stocks common over-the-counter medications for students.
11. Students with chronic conditions must register with the disability services office.
12. Appointments can be scheduled online or by calling the front desk.
13. A doctor's note is required for absences longer than three days.
14. The health center provides free flu shots at the beginning of each semester.
15. All medical records are kept strictly confidential under privacy regulations.

16. The gym is open daily from six in the morning until midnight.
17. Students must swipe their campus ID to enter the facility.
18. Personal training sessions can be booked at the front desk.
19. Group fitness classes are offered at no additional cost.
20. Lockers are available for daily rental with a small deposit.
21. Equipment must be wiped down after each use.
22. Weightlifting belts and straps are available to borrow from staff.
23. The swimming pool is reserved for lap swimming on weekday mornings.
24. Towels are provided at the towel exchange station near the entrance.
25. Sports equipment can be checked out for up to four hours.
26. The indoor track is open for walking and jogging at all hours.
27. Court reservations for tennis and basketball must be made in advance.
28. The gym offers a free orientation session for first-time users.
29. Guests may use the facility with a registered student for a daily fee.
30. Proper athletic footwear is required in all exercise areas.

32. Guests must sign in at the front desk before visiting.
33. Cooking is only permitted in the designated kitchen areas.

37. Packages can be picked up at the mail room during business hours.
38. Pets are not allowed in university-owned residential buildings.
39. All residents must complete a room condition form upon moving in.
40. Noise complaints should be reported to the residential advisor on duty.
41. Smoking is prohibited inside all campus residential buildings.
42. Roommate conflicts should first be addressed with the floor advisor.

45. Residents are encouraged to participate in floor meetings held each month.
