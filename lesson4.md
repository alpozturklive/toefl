## Tema 1: Sağlık Merkezi (Health Center)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Kampüs sağlık merkezi; resepsiyon, bekleme alanı, eczane penceresi.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Walk-in appointments are available on weekday mornings.
2. **(8 sn)** Students must present their insurance cards at check-in.
3. **(10 sn)** Prescription refills can be requested through the online portal.
4. **(10 sn)** All medical records are kept strictly confidential under privacy regulations.
5. **(10 sn)** Mental health counseling is offered free of charge to enrolled students.
6. **(12 sn)** A doctor's note is required for absences longer than three days.
7. **(12 sn)** The health center provides free flu shots at the beginning of each semester.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. The health center is closed on university holidays.
9. Vaccinations are required before moving into campus housing.
10. Annual physical exams are recommended for all students.
11. Test results are typically available within three to five business days.
12. Emergency cases should be directed to the nearest hospital emergency room.
13. The pharmacy stocks common over-the-counter medications for students.
14. Students with chronic conditions must register with the disability services office.
15. Appointments can be scheduled online or by calling the front desk.

## Tema 2: Spor Salonu (Campus Gym)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Kampüs spor salonu; ağırlık aletleri, koşu bandı, giriş turnikesi.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Equipment must be wiped down after each use.
2. **(8 sn)** Group fitness classes are offered at no additional cost.
3. **(10 sn)** Weightlifting belts and straps are available to borrow from staff.
4. **(10 sn)** The gym offers a free orientation session for first-time users.
5. **(10 sn)** The swimming pool is reserved for lap swimming on weekday mornings.
6. **(12 sn)** The indoor track is open for walking and jogging at all hours.
7. **(12 sn)** Guests may use the facility with a registered student for a daily fee.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. The gym is open daily from six in the morning until midnight.
9. Students must swipe their campus ID to enter the facility.
10. Personal training sessions can be booked at the front desk.
11. Lockers are available for daily rental with a small deposit.
12. Towels are provided at the towel exchange station near the entrance.
13. Sports equipment can be checked out for up to four hours.
14. Court reservations for tennis and basketball must be made in advance.
15. Proper athletic footwear is required in all exercise areas.

## Tema 3: Yurt Hayatı (Dorm Life)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Bir öğrenci yurdu; ortak mutfak, çamaşırhane, kat koridoru.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Quiet hours begin at ten o'clock on weeknights.
2. **(8 sn)** Residents are responsible for keeping common areas clean.
3. **(10 sn)** Guests must sign in at the front desk before visiting.
4. **(10 sn)** Laundry facilities are located in the basement of each building.
5. **(10 sn)** Roommate conflicts should first be addressed with the floor advisor.
6. **(12 sn)** Packages can be picked up at the mail room during business hours.
7. **(12 sn)** Students must vacate their rooms within twenty-four hours of the semester ending.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Cooking is only permitted in the designated kitchen areas.
9. Maintenance requests can be submitted through the housing portal.
10. Pets are not allowed in university-owned residential buildings.
11. All residents must complete a room condition form upon moving in.
12. Noise complaints should be reported to the residential advisor on duty.
13. Smoking is prohibited inside all campus residential buildings.
14. Common areas are available for group study sessions and social events.
15. Residents are encouraged to participate in floor meetings held each month.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma. AI bunları sayar.
2. **Thought Groups** — "Although", "Because" sonrası ve virgülde doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" kelimelerini vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme (books ≠ book).
5. **Düz tonlama** — Robot gibi konuşma; İngilizce ritimli bir dildir.
