## Tema 1: Oryantasyon Haftası (Orientation Week)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Oryantasyon haftası; kayıt masası, isim kartları, kampüs turu.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** New students gather in the main auditorium.
2. **(8 sn)** Students receive their class schedules during orientation.
3. **(10 sn)** Orientation leaders will answer your questions throughout the week.
4. **(10 sn)** Academic advisors help new students finalize their course selections.
5. **(10 sn)** First-year students must complete an online safety module before classes.
6. **(12 sn)** The orientation packet includes a campus map and important phone numbers.
7. **(12 sn)** The week ends with a welcome ceremony hosted by the university president.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Name tags are provided at the registration table.
9. The campus tour begins outside the student center.
10. A welcome lunch is served in the dining hall at noon.
11. The library tour introduces students to research resources.
12. Information about clubs and organizations is shared at the activities fair.
13. Students are encouraged to attend every orientation session.
14. Group activities help new students get to know their classmates.
15. Attending orientation helps students feel prepared for their first semester.

## Tema 2: Lisansüstü Başvuru (Graduate Admissions)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Lisansüstü başvuru ofisi; başvuru belgeleri, danışma masası.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Three letters of recommendation are required.
2. **(8 sn)** Funding opportunities include assistantships and fellowships.
3. **(10 sn)** Research experience strengthens a graduate school application.
4. **(10 sn)** Applicants should contact potential advisors before applying.
5. **(10 sn)** Applicants must submit official transcripts from each institution.
6. **(12 sn)** A complete application includes a resume and a writing sample.
7. **(12 sn)** Accepted students must confirm their enrollment by the stated deadline.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. The application deadline is in early December.
9. A statement of purpose is part of the application.
10. The admissions committee reviews each application carefully.
11. Standardized test scores must be sent directly to the university.
12. Interviews are conducted for shortlisted candidates only.
13. International applicants must demonstrate English proficiency.
14. The program admits a limited number of students each year.
15. Admission decisions are typically announced in early spring.

## Tema 3: Kampüs Sürdürülebilirliği (Campus Sustainability)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Kampüs sürdürülebilirlik alanı; geri dönüşüm kutuları, güneş panelleri.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Recycling bins are placed throughout the campus.
2. **(8 sn)** The university encourages students to save energy.
3. **(10 sn)** Many campus buildings are certified for energy efficiency.
4. **(10 sn)** Reusable water bottles are available at the campus store.
5. **(10 sn)** Students can join the environmental club to get involved.
6. **(12 sn)** Students are reminded to turn off electronics when not in use.
7. **(12 sn)** Reducing waste is a shared responsibility of the entire campus community.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Solar panels provide power for several campus buildings.
9. The cafeteria composts food waste from every meal.
10. Bike-sharing stations are located near the residence halls.
11. The campus aims to reduce its carbon footprint each year.
12. Lights in classrooms are equipped with motion sensors.
13. The university hosts a sustainability fair every Earth Day.
14. Native plants are used in landscaping to conserve water.
15. The shuttle service runs on electric buses to reduce emissions.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma. AI bunları sayar.
2. **Thought Groups** — "Although", "Because" sonrası ve virgülde doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" kelimelerini vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme (books ≠ book).
5. **Düz tonlama** — Robot gibi konuşma; İngilizce ritimli bir dildir.
