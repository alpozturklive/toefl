## Tema 1: Campus Fitness Center

1. Welcome to the fitness center.
2. Please scan your student ID at the entrance.
3. Athletic shoes must be worn at all times on the gym floor.
4. Wipe down every machine after you finish using it.
5. Group exercise classes are held on the second floor each evening.
6. If you need help with the free weights, ask a personal trainer for guidance.
7. Lockers are available in the changing rooms, but you must bring your own lock.
8. Only water bottles with secure lids are allowed in the workout area.
9. Group fitness classes require registration through the campus app.
10. Equipment such as resistance bands can be checked out at the front desk.
11. Please return all weights to their proper racks before leaving the facility.
12. Personal training sessions can be scheduled online at a discounted student rate.
13. The pool is located on the ground floor and opens at six in the morning.
14. Swim lanes must be reserved online at least one day in advance.
15. The fitness center closes at eleven on weeknights and at eight on weekends.

## Tema 2: Community Sports Center

16. Thank you for joining the campus sports center.
17. Please wear clean, non-marking shoes inside all courts.
18. The swimming pool is on the ground floor of the building.
19. Members must wipe down equipment after every use.
20. Group fitness classes run every evening of the week.
21. To play tennis, reserve a court online at least twenty-four hours ahead.
22. Store your personal items in the locker room to keep them safe.
23. Basketball courts can be reserved online up to forty-eight hours in advance.
24. Lockers are available for daily use and require a coin deposit.
25. If you lose your membership card, a replacement can be printed at the front desk.
26. Group sports leagues are organized each semester through the intramural office.
27. Guests may use the facility for a daily fee when accompanied by a member.
28. All members must sign a liability waiver before using the climbing wall.
29. The indoor track is open for general use when no events are scheduled.
30. Childcare services are available in the east wing during morning hours.

## Tema 3: Campus Recreation Programs

31. Welcome to the campus recreation center.
32. Please sign in at the front desk each time you visit.
33. The indoor pool opens at six-thirty on weekday mornings.
34. Lockers are available for daily use only — do not leave items overnight.
35. All group fitness classes require advance registration through the app.
36. If you plan to use the weight room, bring a clean towel with you.
37. Indoor basketball courts can be reserved online up to two days in advance.
38. The recreation center offers yoga, spin, and aqua aerobics each week.
39. Outdoor adventure trips are organized monthly through the rec office.
40. Kayak and camping gear can be rented from the outdoor equipment room.
41. Weekend climbing trips are posted on the recreation center bulletin board.
42. Intramural sports registration opens at the beginning of each semester.
43. All participants in contact sports must wear the provided protective gear.
44. The recreation staff can help you find an activity that fits your schedule.
45. Student recreation memberships are included in the semester activity fee.
