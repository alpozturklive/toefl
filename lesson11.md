## Senaryo 1: Campus Fitness Center — Entry

1. Welcome to the fitness center.
2. Please scan your student ID.
3. Wear athletic shoes at all times.
4. Wipe down every machine after you finish using it.
5. Group exercise classes are held on the second floor each evening.
6. If you need help with the free weights, please ask one of the personal trainers for guidance.
7. Lockers are available in the changing rooms, but you must bring and provide your own padlock.

---

## Senaryo 2: Campus Fitness Center — Rules & Classes

8. Only water bottles are allowed inside.
9. Return all weights to the racks.
10. The pool opens at six each morning.
11. Group fitness classes require advance registration through the campus app.
12. Personal training sessions are available at a discounted rate for all enrolled students.
13. Swim lanes in the pool must be reserved online at least one full day before your visit.
14. The fitness center closes at eleven on weeknights and at eight o'clock on weekend evenings.

---

## Senaryo 3: Community Sports Center — Welcome

15. Thank you for joining our sports center.
16. Please wear clean shoes indoors.
17. Show your membership card each visit.
18. The swimming pool is located on the ground floor of this building.
19. Members are required to wipe down all equipment after every use.
20. To reserve a tennis court, you must book online at least twenty-four hours in advance.
21. Personal items should always be stored in the locker room to keep them safe while you work out.

---

## Senaryo 4: Community Sports Center — Courts & Classes

22. Group classes run every evening.
23. Lockers need a coin deposit.
24. Guests pay a small daily fee.
25. Basketball courts must be reserved online up to forty-eight hours in advance.
26. If you lose your membership card, a replacement can be printed at the front desk.
27. Group sports leagues for intramural teams are organized at the start of each new academic semester.
28. All members must sign a liability waiver before using the indoor climbing wall for the first time.

---

## Senaryo 5: Recreation Center — Programs

29. Welcome to the recreation center.
30. Please sign in at the front desk.
31. The indoor pool opens at six-thirty.
32. All group fitness classes require advance registration through the official campus app.
33. If you plan to use the weight room, remember to bring a clean towel with you.
34. Indoor basketball courts can be reserved online up to two days before the date you want to play.
35. The recreation center offers yoga, spin cycling, and aqua aerobics classes throughout each week of the semester.

---

## Senaryo 6: Recreation Center — Outdoor Adventures

36. Camping gear is available to rent.
37. Outdoor trips are posted monthly.
38. Bring your student ID to borrow.
39. Kayaks and climbing equipment can be rented from the outdoor gear room on the lower level.
40. Intramural sports registration opens at the very beginning of each new semester for all students.
41. Participants in all contact sports are required to wear the protective gear provided by the recreation staff.
42. Student recreation memberships are included automatically in the standard activity fee charged each semester.
