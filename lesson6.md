## Tema 1: Öğrenci Toplulukları (Student Organizations)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Öğrenci kulüpleri tanıtım masaları; afişler, kayıt formları, gönüllüler.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Fundraising activities require prior approval from campus administration.
2. **(8 sn)** Club membership is open to all currently enrolled students.
3. **(10 sn)** Inactive clubs risk losing their funding and official recognition status.
4. **(10 sn)** Volunteer opportunities are posted on the student affairs bulletin board.
5. **(10 sn)** Weekly meetings are held every Tuesday evening in the student union.
6. **(12 sn)** The student government allocates funds to approved clubs on a competitive basis.
7. **(12 sn)** All student organizations must renew their registration at the start of each year.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Applications for leadership positions are due at the end of the semester.
9. New members must attend an orientation session before joining.
10. The club requires a minimum of ten active members to remain registered.
11. Budget proposals must be submitted to the student government office.
12. Community service hours are required for club recognition each year.
13. Events must be approved by the student activities office in advance.
14. Club officers are elected by the general membership each spring.
15. The student newspaper publishes a calendar of all registered club events.

## Tema 2: Kampüs Etkinlikleri (Campus Events)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Bir kampüs etkinliği; sahne, sandalyeler, katılan öğrenciler.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Event volunteers receive community service credit toward graduation requirements.
2. **(8 sn)** Weather-related cancellations are announced through the university alert system.
3. **(10 sn)** All campus events must comply with the university noise ordinance.
4. **(10 sn)** Photography and recording may be restricted at certain campus performances.
5. **(10 sn)** The cultural festival features food, music, and performances from around the world.
6. **(12 sn)** Free admission to campus museum exhibits is available with a student ID.
7. **(12 sn)** Tickets for the spring concert are sold at the student union box office.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. The annual orientation week begins three days before classes start.
9. Commencement ceremonies are held in the main auditorium each May.
10. Academic lectures open to the public are announced on the campus website.
11. Parking is limited during large campus events, so public transit is recommended.
12. The student union hosts a weekly open mic night on Friday evenings.
13. Campus tours for prospective students are offered Monday through Saturday.
14. Holiday events are organized by the international student services office.
15. The career fair is held twice a year in the main gymnasium.

## Tema 3: Yurt Dışında Eğitim (Study Abroad)
> 🖼️ **Sahne:** Yurt dışı eğitim ofisi; harita, program broşürleri, danışman.

### 🎯 Sınav Seti — kısa→uzun, sınav gibi sırayla (süreyle çalış)
1. **(8 sn)** Credit transfer policies vary depending on the host institution.
2. **(8 sn)** The international office offers pre-departure orientation sessions each semester.
3. **(10 sn)** Students are required to obtain health insurance coverage while abroad.
4. **(10 sn)** Financial aid can often be applied toward approved study abroad programs.
5. **(10 sn)** Students must register their travel plans with the university safety office.
6. **(12 sn)** A valid passport is required at least six months before the departure date.
7. **(12 sn)** Some programs require students to take courses taught entirely in the local language.

### 📚 Ekstra Pratik (zamansız tekrar)
8. Applications for study abroad programs are accepted twice a year.
9. Students must maintain a minimum GPA to be eligible for exchange programs.
10. Language proficiency requirements differ by program and destination country.
11. Housing arrangements are typically coordinated through the host university.
12. A study abroad advisor can help students select the most suitable program.
13. Internship credit may be earned through approved international placements.
14. Re-entry advising is available to help students readjust after returning to campus.
15. Scholarships specifically for study abroad are listed on the financial aid website.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma. AI bunları sayar.
2. **Thought Groups** — "Although", "Because" sonrası ve virgülde doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" kelimelerini vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme (books ≠ book).
5. **Düz tonlama** — Robot gibi konuşma; İngilizce ritimli bir dildir.
