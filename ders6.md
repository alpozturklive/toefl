## Tema 1: Öğrenci Toplulukları (Student Organizations)

1. Club membership is open to all currently enrolled students.
2. Applications for leadership positions are due at the end of the semester.
3. Weekly meetings are held every Tuesday evening in the student union.
4. New members must attend an orientation session before joining.
5. The club requires a minimum of ten active members to remain registered.
6. Budget proposals must be submitted to the student government office.
7. Community service hours are required for club recognition each year.
8. Events must be approved by the student activities office in advance.
9. Club officers are elected by the general membership each spring.
10. Fundraising activities require prior approval from campus administration.
11. The student newspaper publishes a calendar of all registered club events.
12. Inactive clubs risk losing their funding and official recognition status.
13. All student organizations must renew their registration at the start of each year.
14. Volunteer opportunities are posted on the student affairs bulletin board.
15. The student government allocates funds to approved clubs on a competitive basis.

23. Campus tours for prospective students are offered Monday through Saturday.
24. Holiday events are organized by the international student services office.
25. All campus events must comply with the university noise ordinance.
26. Photography and recording may be restricted at certain campus performances.

30. Weather-related cancellations are announced through the university alert system.

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma.
2. **Thought Groups** — virgülden sonra doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme.
5. **Düz tonlama** — cümle sonunda doğal iniş-çıkış yap.
