## Senaryo 1: Chemistry Lab — Safety Basics

1. Please put on your safety goggles.
2. Food and drinks are not allowed.
3. Tie back your long hair.
4. Wash your hands thoroughly before leaving the laboratory area.
5. All chemical spills must be reported to the lab instructor immediately.
6. Broken glassware must be placed in the designated sharps container near the main exit door.
7. You are required to wear a lab coat and closed-toe shoes throughout the entire lab session.

---

## Senaryo 2: Chemistry Lab — Procedures

8. Label every sample container clearly.
9. Use only mechanical pipettes here.
10. Report any unusual odors quickly.
11. Review the safety data sheet before working with any new chemical substance.
12. Dispose of all chemical waste in the correct labeled bins posted above each station.
13. The ventilation system must be running at full capacity whenever you work with volatile chemical substances.
14. Never return unused chemicals to the original container — dispose of them following the posted waste guidelines.

---

## Senaryo 3: Biology Lab — Safety Rules

15. Goggles must be worn at all times.
16. Tie back hair before handling specimens.
17. Always wash hands before leaving.
18. Broken glass goes directly into the red sharps bin near the exit.
19. If you spill a chemical, step back and notify the lab instructor right away.
20. Make sure all microscopes are properly turned off and unplugged before you leave at the end of class.
21. Biological materials and waste must be disposed of only in the clearly marked biohazard bags at each station.

---

## Senaryo 4: Biology Lab — Procedures

22. Record all data in ink only.
23. Do not eat at your workstation.
24. Students work in pairs here.
25. Complete the online safety quiz before attending your very first lab session.
26. Return all reagents to their proper labeled storage locations immediately after you finish using them.
27. Lab reports must be reviewed and signed off by the supervising instructor before you are allowed to leave.
28. The lab is open for independent research only during the posted hours listed on the department website.

---

## Senaryo 5: Robotics Open House — Welcome

29. Welcome to the robotics open house.
30. Leave your backpacks by the door.
31. Wear safety glasses inside the lab.
32. Please do not touch any equipment or student projects on the display tables.
33. Feel free to speak with the student engineers about their ongoing research and current projects.
34. If you are interested in joining the team, please speak with the lead researcher after the tour concludes.
35. After exploring the main demonstration floor, the group will move together to the software design and coding room.

---

## Senaryo 6: Robotics Open House — Club & Activities

36. The club meets every Wednesday evening.
37. All majors are welcome to join.
38. Photography is allowed in the main hall.
39. Membership applications open at the very start of each new academic semester each year.
40. The club competes in both regional and national robotics competitions throughout the academic year.
41. Sponsors and industry partners from local technology companies are often present at our open house events.
42. Please sign the attendance sheet near the entrance today to receive your official participation credit for this visit.
