## Tema 1: Chemistry Lab Safety

1. Please put on your safety goggles before entering the lab.
2. Food and drinks are never allowed inside the laboratory.
3. All chemical spills must be reported to the instructor immediately.
4. Wash your hands thoroughly before leaving the lab area.
5. Broken glass must be placed in the designated sharps container.
6. You are required to wear a lab coat and closed-toe shoes at all times.
7. In an emergency, use the eye wash station near the front exit.
8. Store all chemical reagents in their proper labeled containers after use.
9. Ventilation must be running whenever you work with volatile substances.
10. Label every sample container clearly with your name and the date.
11. Dispose of chemical waste in the correct bins posted above each station.
12. Review the safety data sheet for any new chemical before you begin.
13. Tie back long hair and remove loose jewelry before starting an experiment.
14. Never pipette by mouth — always use a mechanical pipetting device.
15. Report any unusual odors or equipment malfunctions to the lab supervisor.

## Tema 2: Biology Lab Safety

16. Safety goggles must be worn at all times in this laboratory.
17. Tie back long hair before handling any specimens or equipment.
18. Always wash your hands thoroughly before leaving the room.
19. Broken glass should go directly in the red sharps bin near the door.
20. Do not eat or drink at your workstations under any circumstances.
21. If you spill a chemical, notify the instructor immediately and step back.
22. Make sure all microscopes are turned off and unplugged before you leave.
23. Gloves are provided at the supply station near the main entrance.
24. Results must be reviewed and signed by the lab instructor before you leave.
25. The lab is open for independent research only during posted hours.
26. Return all reagents to their proper storage locations after use.
27. Students are paired in groups of two for all experimental procedures.
28. Record all data in the official lab notebook using ink, not pencil.
29. Dispose of biological materials in the labeled biohazard bags only.
30. Complete the online safety quiz before attending your first lab session.

## Tema 3: Robotics Lab Open House

31. Welcome to the robotics open house.
32. Please leave your backpacks near the door when you enter.
33. You must wear safety glasses inside the testing and demonstration area.
34. Please do not touch any equipment or projects on the display tables.
35. Feel free to ask the student engineers about their current projects.
36. If you are interested in joining the team, speak with the lead researcher.
37. After the main floor tour, we will move to the software design room.
38. The robotics club meets every Wednesday evening in this building.
39. Membership applications open at the start of each academic semester.
40. Students from any major are welcome to join the engineering team.
41. The club competes in regional and national robotics competitions each year.
42. Sponsors and industry partners are often present at open house events.
43. Photography of the demonstration robots is permitted in the main hall only.
44. Refreshments are available in the lobby after the guided tour ends.
45. Sign the attendance sheet near the entrance to receive your participation credit.
