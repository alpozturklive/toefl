2. Students must meet with their advisor before registering for classes.
3. A hold on your account will prevent you from enrolling in courses.
4. Official transcripts can be requested through the registrar's online portal.
5. The deadline to add or drop a course is the end of the second week.
6. Withdrawing from a course after the deadline results in a W on your transcript.
7. Enrollment verification letters are available at the registrar's office upon request.
8. Degree audit reports show exactly which graduation requirements have been fulfilled.
9. Name and address changes must be submitted in writing to the registrar's office.
10. A minimum of twelve credit hours is required for full-time student status.
11. Incomplete grades must be resolved within one semester of the original course ending.
12. Students may not enroll in more than eighteen credit hours without written approval.
13. Final grades are posted to the student portal within two weeks after the semester ends.
14. Transfer credits are evaluated jointly by the registrar and the relevant academic department.
15. The official academic calendar lists all important registration dates and deadlines for the year.

24. Transfer students should schedule an advising appointment during their very first week on campus.
25. The academic advising center is open Monday through Friday during regular business hours.
26. Students may request a different advisor if they change their major or area of concentration.
27. Course substitutions and waivers must be reviewed and approved by the advising office.

## Puanlama (Sınav Rubriği)

| Puan | Açıklama |
|------|----------|
| **5** | Tam anlaşılır. Eksik kelime yok. Doğal vurgu ve tonlama. |
| **4** | Küçük hatalar (article, edat). Anlam korunmuş. |
| **3** | Bazı anahtar kelimeler eksik veya değişmiş. |
| **2** | Cümlenin büyük kısmı eksik. Anlaşılması zor. |
| **1** | Sadece birkaç kelime. Cümle bütünlüğü yok. |
| **0** | Yanıt yok. |

**Süre sınırları:**
- Kısa cümleler (1-2 kelime grubu): **8 saniye**
- Orta cümleler (3-5 kelime grubu): **10 saniye**
- Uzun cümleler (6-7 kelime grubu): **12 saniye**

## 5 Telaffuz Tuzağı

1. **Article Trap** — "a, an, the" kelimelerini yutma.
2. **Thought Groups** — virgülden sonra doğal duraklama yap.
3. **Function Words** — "is, at, for, of" vurgulama ama yutma da.
4. **Sondaki ekler** — "-s" ve "-ed" eklerini düşürme.
5. **Düz tonlama** — cümle sonunda doğal iniş-çıkış yap.
